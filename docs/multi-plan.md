# Multi Plan

This file is the active source of truth for the `Multi` direction.

## Product role

`Multi` is the future resonance layer of `Days`.

It is not the current proving surface.

## Current status

`Multi` stays visible as a product direction, but implementation remains intentionally secondary.

Current goal:
- describe it clearly
- avoid fake depth
- keep it from distorting `Start` and `Single`

## Working direction

Possible long-term movement:
- signals feed areas
- areas feed `Single`
- `Single` reveals reading and response patterns
- those patterns form the basis of `Profil`
- `Profil` may later support recommendation, matching, or resonance

This direction is still exploratory and must remain optional, careful, and non-creepy.

## Guardrails

- no manipulative language
- no pseudo-magical claims
- no assumption that matching is always good
- no hidden outward projection of profile data
- consent and clarity must matter later

## Short-term plan

- keep `Multi` compile-safe
- document the ambition
- do not overbuild the runtime yet
- let terminology evolve after `Start` and `Single` are stable
